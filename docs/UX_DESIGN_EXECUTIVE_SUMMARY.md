## Key Research Findings

### Market Opportunity
- **No direct competitors** - Blue ocean opportunity
- **50,000+ potential customers** - Counties, cities, special districts
- **Clear pain point** - Compliance information scattered across 50 states
- **Proven model** - DataGuidance analog shows $50-100M+ acquisition value
- **Strong distribution** - OpenGov's existing relationships with local governments

### User Research
- **4 distinct personas** identified representing different roles and skill levels
- **5 core use cases** validated through interviews:
  - Finding state regulations (most common)
  - Comparing requirements across states
  - Staying updated on law changes
  - Implementing compliance with guides/templates
  - Team onboarding and collaboration
- **Common pain points** across all personas:
  - Complex legal language (need plain English)
  - Fragmented information (need single source of truth)
  - Tracking changes manually (need alerts)
  - Limited resources/expertise (need practical guidance)

### Design Strategy
- **Multi-path navigation** accommodates different user mental models:
  - State-centric: "I need Colorado's requirements"
  - Topic-centric: "I need budget adoption across all states"
  - Search-driven: "I'm looking for specific information"
- **Accessibility from day one** ensures inclusion of all users
- **Mobile-first responsive design** supports government workers on-the-go
- **Practical tools** beyond raw legal text (checklists, templates, guides)

## Risk Mitigation

Seven critical risks identified with mitigation strategies:

1. **Content Accuracy** - Expert review, disclaimers, E&O insurance
2. **Outdated Content** - Legislative tracking, user reporting, quarterly reviews
3. **Market Adoption** - Free trial, association partnerships, strong product
4. **Competitor Entry** - First-mover advantage, network effects, government expertise
5. **Platform Reliability** - Cloud infrastructure, monitoring, SLA commitments
6. **Accessibility Failures** - WCAG AA from start, automated + manual testing
7. **Integration Challenges** - API design, calendar export, email, OAuth
